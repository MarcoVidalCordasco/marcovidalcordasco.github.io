About me

Post-doc at the EvoAdapta group, University of Cantabria.
Strong advocate for open data and code.
Always curious.

Ecosystems are not merely static backdrops for human activity, but dynamic and complex systems in which humans are active agents, often leaving long-term legacies across the terrestrial biosphere. I am interested in understanding these dynamics during the Palaeolithic by integrating archaeological data (such as archaeozoology, palynology, sedaDNA or biomarkers) into computational approaches.
