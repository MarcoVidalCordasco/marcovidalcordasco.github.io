About me

Ecosystems are not merely static backdrops for human activity, but dynamic and complex systems in which humans are active agents, often leaving long-term legacies across the terrestrial biosphere. I am interested in reconstructing these dynamics during the Palaeolithic by integrating archaeological data into computational approaches.
