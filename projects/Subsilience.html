Subsilience

2021–2025 – Subsistence and human resilience to sudden climatic events in Europe during MIS3

Funding: European Research Council – €2,000,000

Principal Investigator: Ana B. Marín-Arroyo

← Back to project list

Project Overview

This project investigates how human populations during the Middle and Upper Paleolithic in Europe responded to abrupt climatic events during Marine Isotope Stage 3 (MIS3).

Objectives

  • Analyze archaeological evidence of resilience and human adaptation.
  • Reconstruct paleoenvironments and subsistence strategies.
  • Compare cultural responses across different European regions.
